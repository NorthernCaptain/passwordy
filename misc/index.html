Passwordy - Offline Password Manager

Available on Multiple Platforms

Passwordy is an offline password manager available for all your devices:

  • Android
  • iOS
  • macOS
  • Linux
  • Windows

Securely Store Your Secrets

Passwordy allows you to store various secrets, including websites, passwords, and other sensitive information, in a secure, locally encrypted database. Your secrets are protected using strong encryption, ensuring that they are safe from unauthorized access.

Sync Across All Your Devices

With Passwordy, you can sync your encrypted database across all your devices using your own Google Drive. This means that:

  • There are no external servers involved – your data is never stored on any server except your Google Drive.
  • Your secrets are only yours and are never shared with any other service or server.
  • You maintain full control over your data and where it is stored.

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Passwordy does not collect any user or device information. We do not gather any usage statistics or analytics. Your privacy is fully respected, and you can use Passwordy with confidence, knowing that your data remains private and secure.

Open Source and Transparent

Passwordy is an open-source application, meaning the source code is available for anyone to inspect, modify, and contribute to. You can find the source code on GitHub:

https://github.com/NorthernCaptain/passwordy

We believe in transparency and the power of community-driven development, and we invite you to be a part of the Passwordy community.
